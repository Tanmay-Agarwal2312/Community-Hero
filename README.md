Community Hero is a hyperlocal civic issue reporting platform that lets citizens report infrastructure problems — potholes, broken streetlights, water leaks, and waste management issues — directly from their phone. Reports are automatically categorized using Google's Gemini vision model, pinned to a live map, and routed through a transparent five-stage resolution pipeline. Community upvoting verifies new reports and confirms completed fixes, while a built-in duplicate-detection system prevents the same issue from being reported repeatedly. An impact dashboard surfaces resolution trends and category breakdowns, giving citizens and municipal authorities a shared, accountable view of neighborhood infrastructure health.
